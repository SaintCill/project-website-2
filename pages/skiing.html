Måttsunds berget

Located about 20 km from central Luleå, Måttsunds berget offers slopes for both beginners and experienced skiers. Måttsund has something for everyone, even complete beginners, as you can join the beginner classes offered at Måttsund.

Ormberget outdoor center

Nestled just 4km from central Luleå, Ormberget outdoor center offers more than just slopes. Not only does Ormberget have beginner friendly slopes, but it also offers a biathlon track. Want to take a break from the thrill of downhill skiing? Ormberget also has some great cross-country tracks. Want to slow down and take in the scenery? Not only does Ormberget offer a great view, but it is also home to many adventure trails! For those wanting the full outdoor experience, Ormberget also offers several outdoor barbeque areas.

Other skiing opportunities

There are of course other options if neither of the two previous options tickle your fancy. Mjölkuddsberget and Bergnäset are both a stones throw from central Luleå. If you really want though, you could just take your skis and simply head out into the wilderness. The swedish forests are often well traveled even during winter months, and you would be hard pressed not to find at least one trail wherever you decide to go. We do recommend making sure to tell someone where you are planning on going, if you decide to just head out into wilderness. Safety should be your first priority, and we wouldn't recommend heading out into unknown territory alone. A friend, a shovel and a satellite phone go a long way in making sure that you return safe and sound.
